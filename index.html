Our Story

Where Every Fabric
Tells a Story

Located in the heart of Idumota, Lagos Island, Mercyked Collections is Lagos' trusted destination for premium Aso-Ebi fabrics. From intimate ceremonies to grand owambe celebrations, we dress Nigeria's most memorable moments in style.

Every piece is carefully sourced, from the intricate detail of Cord Lace to the dazzling shimmer of Swarovski Velvet. We proudly serve both retail buyers and wholesale clients, men and women, with the same dedication to excellence.
